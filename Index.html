On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous

On Earth We're Briefly Gorgeous by Ocean Vuong follows the protagonist, Little Dog, in letter format through a letter that she writes to her illiterate mom. The book goes through Little Dog's every experience, from growing up a Vietnamese immigrant with a mom who's school got bombed in the war when she was five, to her most vulnerable thoughts and actions. June 4, 2019.

Ocean Vuong is a Vietnamese poet with a powerful description style. He was born in Vietnam but grew up in America.

10/10.
